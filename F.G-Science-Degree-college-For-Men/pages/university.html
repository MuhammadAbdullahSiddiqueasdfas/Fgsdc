About National University of Pakistan

The National University of Pakistan (NUP) stands as a beacon of higher education in the country, committed to providing world-class academic programs and fostering an environment of research and innovation. Established with the vision to create leaders and thinkers, NUP has consistently ranked among the top universities in Pakistan.

With its state-of-the-art facilities, distinguished faculty, and comprehensive curriculum, NUP offers students an unparalleled educational experience. The university's emphasis on practical learning and industry collaboration ensures that graduates are well-prepared to meet the challenges of the modern world.

NUP's affiliation with F.G Science Degree College brings this excellence to Wah Cantt, allowing our students to benefit from quality education while studying in their hometown. Our BS programs in Computer Science and Physics are meticulously designed to meet NUP's rigorous academic standards.

The university's dynamic learning environment, coupled with its commitment to research and development, makes it an ideal choice for students seeking comprehensive higher education. NUP's degree programs are recognized nationally and internationally, opening doors to global opportunities for our graduates.
